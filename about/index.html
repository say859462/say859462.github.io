👋 Introduction

Hi, I'm Jerry Liu (劉哲良), currently a graduate student at NYCU in the CAIG Lab.

Back in college, friends used to call me 麻吉, 馬鈴薯 (in Taiwanese), or 麻古. These nicknames came from my online name Mosmochi and the fact that I often jokingly called someone 麻吉 (meaning "buddy"). Over time, I naturally became everyone's 麻吉.

I really enjoy playing video games, especially single-player titles.

Recently, I've been trying to build a reading habit. I’m particularly interested in books about:

  • Biographies

As for sports, I'm more into badminton and volleyball.
